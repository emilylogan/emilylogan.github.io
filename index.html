Emily Logan

Emily Logan has lived in Spokane Washington her whole life. When she’s not busy with work or school life, Emily enjoys cooking and baking for her friends and family. Currently, Emily is working two jobs. Starting in September of 2019 she began working as a caretaker and in December 2019 she began working at Schweitzer Engineering Laboratories as an Editor Intern.

Before working at her two current jobs, Emily also gained experience as a front-end courtesy clerk at Fred Meyer. This position taught her how to give quality customer service to the people in her community. Before attending Eastern Washington University (EWU), she spent time studying at Spokane Community College (SCC) from September 2016 to June of 2018. After her time at SCC, Emily obtained an associates of arts (AA) at the age of 17. To this day, gaining an AA at 17 is one of Emily’s greatest accomplishments.

In September of 2018 Emily enrolled at EWU and is currently working towards completing a bachelors of technical communication. Emily is a hardworking student. She is also kind, dedicated and patient. Emily looks forward to applying the things she has learned from school as well as previous work experience to all future professional endeavors.
